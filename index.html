What Is Hockey?

Hockey is a full-contact sport played on an ice surface between two teams made up of six players each on the ice three forwards, two defenceman, and one goalie per team. You score goals by shooting the puck into your opponent's net, and the team that has the most goals at the end of 3 periods wins. If you want to play hockey, you will need to get comfortable with the gear and practicing skating quickly and efficiently. With a lot of practice and hard work. You will be able to try all the positions in a hockey game if you please. If you start at a young age you be in half-pint, then you will move up to Novice, Atom, Peewee, Bantam, Midget, Junior, and finally the big leagues,(NHL) AHL) (ECHL).
